ApplicationBioVault

Welcome to BioVault. At BioVault, your security is not an add-on feature: it is the foundation upon which we build every line of code. This Privacy Policy clearly and comprehensively describes how we manage your information when you use our mobile application for managing passwords and sensitive data.

This document has been drafted to strictly comply with the Google Play Developer Program Policies, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), and international digital privacy best practices.

By downloading, installing, or using BioVault, you confirm that you have read, understood, and accepted the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with any part of this document, we request that you stop using the application and delete your account.

Zero-Knowledge Architecture Zero-Knowledge

  • Your passwords and secrets are encrypted locally on your device before being transmitted or stored on our servers.
  • BioVault never has access to your master key nor can it decrypt the contents of your vault under any circumstances.
  • You are the sole owner of your data. Without your key, the data is cryptographically inaccessible to any third party, including our team.
  • In the event of losing your master key, there is no recovery mechanism that compromises your encryption. Security is absolute and irreversible.
02

Information We Collect

BioVault applies the principle of data minimization. We only store the information strictly necessary for the operation of the service:

Data typeDescriptionEncryption
Email addressCollected during registration. Used exclusively for account management, authentication, and notifications.✓ In transit
Vault dataTitles, usernames, and passwords. Stored exclusively in encrypted format (AES-256). BioVault can never read this content.✓ Full E2EE
Biometric dataBioVault uses native OS APIs solely for local authentication. We never collect or store biometric data on our servers.✓ Local only

Information not collected

BioVault does not collect location data, contact lists, browsing history, advertising identifiers (AAID/IDFA), or personally identifiable behavioral analytics.

03

Device Permissions

To provide its core features, BioVault may request access to the following capabilities of your device. No data obtained through these permissions is sent to our servers:

Biometrics (Fingerprint/Face)

Used exclusively to unlock the application locally via the Secure Enclave or Keystore of your device.

Camera (Optional)

Only requested if the user wishes to scan QR codes (e.g., to set up 2FA). Images are not saved or transmitted.

04

How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used exclusively for secure authentication, synchronizing your encrypted data between authorized devices, critical service notifications, and technical support explicitly requested by you.

05

Data Sharing and Providers

BioVault does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties under any circumstances. We use Supabase for secure database storage and user authentication. Supabase complies with SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certifications.

Legal disclosure

We will only share information if there is a binding legal obligation imposed by a competent authority. However, by using a Zero-Knowledge architecture, we cannot hand over or decrypt the contents of your vaults.

06

Data Security

We implement end-to-end encryption (AES-256) before data leaves your device, HTTPS/TLS 1.3 transport protocols, and biometric verification is processed exclusively on your smartphone's secure hardware chip.

07

Data Retention

Your account data (email) and encrypted vault are kept as long as the account remains active. They are immediately and irreversibly deleted at the moment you decide to delete your account.

08

Your Rights as a User

In accordance with the GDPR and international privacy regulations, you have the right to: request access to your data, rectify it, request its deletion (erasure), export it (portability), object to or limit its processing. We address all requests within a maximum legal period of 30 calendar days.

09

Account Deletion

You have the waivable right to delete your account at any time. This is permanent and irreversible.

  • 01 → From the app: Navigate to Settings → Account → Delete account.
  • 02 → By email: Send a request to privacidad@biovault.app.
10

Children's Privacy

BioVault is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum legal age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you are a parent or legal guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at privacidad@biovault.app so we can proceed with the immediate deletion of such account and data from our servers.

11

Cookie Policy

Our mobile application does not use tracking, analytics, or advertising cookies. We are an ad-free and tracker-free service.

12

Changes to this Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy. In the event of any substantial change, we will notify users via a prominent notice within the application or by email, requesting your renewed consent if required by law.

Questions about your privacy?

If you have any questions, concerns, or wish to exercise any of your rights, our privacy team is available to assist you.
